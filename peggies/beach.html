PLAYPEGGIE

Its Summer in Peggie Land and all the Peggies are looking glamorous at the beach. The sun is shining, the sand is warm and the lettuce is floppy. Booties are oversized, expectations are low and hydration is taken very seriously, mostly in the form of crisp, delicious cucumber slices.

Captain Cándido Peggy has taken all the peggies to the beach on his big fancy sail boat. With one paw on the wheel and a firm commitment to looking stylish, Cándido embodies effortless coastal cool. As the boat glides through the water like a peeled cucumber through butter, the Peggies lounge on deck pretending they understand sailing terms.

Anselmo's motto in life is 'chill-ax to the max' and that's exactly what he's doing today. Anselmo has a very hard life. Sipping a tasty carrot smoothie and contemplating absolutely nothing, Anselmo demonstrates peak summer wellness. His current responsibilities include rotating slowly like a sun-warmed chicory and reaching for snacks without sitting up. All the PLAYPEGGIE Peggies agree: no one relaxes quite like Anselmo, and frankly, it shows.

To make sure all the peggies stay safe on the beach David Peggiehoff is keeping guard from his lifeguard tower. From his elevated position, David scans the horizon with the intensity of a Peggie who will notice if a cucumber goes missing. David likes being on the tower because the wind makes his hair look even more epic. The breeze lifts his hair into a state best described as “structurally unsound but heroic.” Safety has never looked this glamorous.

Pamela Andiepeggie is helping David Peggiehoff keep the beach safe. Equipped with style, confidence, and a whistle she occasionally remembers how to use, Pamela brings professionalism and poise to the shoreline. Together, she and David form a dynamic duo, like meadow hay and tissue paper, but legally responsible.

To keep in shape the peggies are playing Volley ball. Its important for the Peggies to maintain their ripped beach bodies. Leaps are attempted, balls are missed and everyone agrees it still counts as exercise. The Peggies believe fitness is about movement, teamwork and rewarding yourself afterward with something leafy. After all, nothing squeaks “summer body” like feeling good, staying hydrated and being at least 60% salad.

PLAYPEGGIE Summer Fashion Special!

This summer, Peggie fashion is all about confidence, ventilation and leafy vegetable accessorising. The modern Peggie knows that true style isn’t about trends, it’s about committing fully to a look, even if that look involves lettuce.

The Must-Have Accessories

Oversized sunglasses remain essential, not just for eye protection but for maintaining an air of mystery while chewing. Straw hats are in, particularly those slightly too big and at risk of falling off in a strong breeze. For evening glamour, nothing elevates an outfit quite like a carefully chosen sprig of parsley worn purely for effect.

Swimwear That Works Harder Than You Do

This season’s swimwear is bold, practical, and unapologetically elasticated. High-waisted shorts, confident bikinis, and anything that says “I may nap at any moment” are all approved. Colours inspired by tasty snacks dominate the palette; carrot orange, cucumber green, and the always daring aubergine.

Statement Vegetables

No Peggie look is complete without a statement vegetable. Think celery as a clutch, radishes threaded like jewellery, or a casually carried corn cob for that effortless “I just threw this on” vibe. Remember: the vegetable should look intentional, even if it will be eaten later.

Footwear: Optional, Attitude Mandatory

Bare paws are back in a big way, though some Peggies are experimenting with novelty flip-flops and anklets made of pea flakes. Comfort is key, if you can’t sprint toward snacks at a moment’s notice, it’s simply not practical

The Final Word Summer fashion in Peggie Land is less about rules and more about feeling good in your own fur. If your outfit flaps slightly in the wind and smells faintly of salad, you’re doing it right.

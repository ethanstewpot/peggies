PLAYPEGGIE

The Peggies’ goal in life is to look fabulous at all times, and today is no exception. Having collectively agreed that sweat is merely another accessory, all the Peggies have headed out to enjoy their favourite sporting activities, fully styled, lightly hydrated, and emotionally supported by snacks. In Peggie Land, sport isn’t just about competition; it’s mostly about commitment to the look.

Bernarda loves to keep in shape by skating around looking glamorous and listing to her favorite tunes. Wearing roller skates that absolutely do not prioritise safety, Bernarda glides effortlessly along the promenade, her walkman blasting pepper ballads and carrot anthems. Her movements are fluid, confident, and mostly intentional. Bernarda doesn’t skate for fitness; she skates for presence.

Petronila and Pancracia love to play tennis, they find it to be the most sophisticated way to play with balls. Dressed in crisp whites and carrying themselves with the seriousness of Peggies who once watched a documentary about Wimbledon, they take their rallies very seriously. Inspired by Bernarda’s fearless glamour, they’ve decided to elevate the experience by attempting tennis on roller skates. What could possibly go wrong? Fashion editors predict chaos, elegance, and at least one dramatic pause for breath and cucumber water.

Meanwhile, Aniceta is leading the peggie Aerobics class. Clad in neon, leg warmers, and motivational optimism, Aniceta counts loudly and confidently, even when no one is quite sure what number they’re on. Arms flail, hips shimmy, and dignity stretches thinner than worn out Lycra. Aniceta reminds everyone that it’s not about perfection; it’s about commitment, coordination, and remembering to look fabulous at all times.

Isidro has decided to work on his peggie pecks, he wants to have the biggest chest in Peggie Land. Isidro approaches his workout with focus, ambition, and absolutely no sense of form. Each lift is followed by a mirror check and a quick snack break. Isidro’s approach to sport is instinctive, treat focused, and refreshingly non-committal. Come back soon to check on his progress!

Severino has been power lifting for as long as he can remember. Severino has a very short memory. Undeterred, he continues lifting with passion, confusion, and mediocre technique. He goal is to come top in the 800g weight class in the next peggie power lifting championships. Severino is definitely the PLAYPEGGIE beefcake of the month!

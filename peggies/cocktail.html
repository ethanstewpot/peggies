Its Cocktail evening in Peggie Land! As the sun sets over Peggie Land, the air is thick with sophistication, carrot cubes clink dramatically, and someone has definitely over-muddled a cucumber. The Peggies have slipped into their finest evening looks, accessorised with confidence, ambition and a suspicious amount of parsley. This is not just cocktail hour this is the PLAYPEGGIE lifestyle.

Encarnación has found a delicious martini and a lovingly curated platter of her favourite veggies, arranged with the care of a Michelin-starred salad bar. There’s fennel for flair, carrot batons for crunch, and a daring hint of radicchio for emotional depth. Encarnación is living the Peggie dream life, gently swirling her drink while contemplating which vegetable truly gets her. Spoiler: it’s celery.

Manolo is making sure all the Peggies stay refreshed with lovely cocktails. After a hard day of looking fabulous, there’s nothing Concepción likes more than taking a well-deserved rest by sipping a sophisticated long drink. Reclining elegantly, Concepción nurses her glass like it’s a trusted old friend, complete with an artisanal cucumber spear and a sprig of dill for drama. She believes rest is essential, hydration is sacred, and cocktails should always contain at least one vegetable pretending to be decorative.
